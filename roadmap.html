Roadmap

Here is the roadmap for future enhancements and features planned for the Solar Panel Dashboard:

Feature 1: Real-time Energy Monitoring

Implement real-time energy monitoring functionality to provide live data on solar panel energy production and consumption.

Feature 2: Energy Efficiency Analytics

Add energy efficiency analytics to provide insights and recommendations for optimizing energy usage and maximizing savings.

Feature 3: Remote Monitoring and Control

Enable remote monitoring and control of solar panel systems, allowing users to monitor and manage their panels from anywhere.

Feature 4: Integration with Smart Home Systems

Integrate the dashboard with smart home systems toallow users to seamlessly control their solar panels alongside other smart home devices and appliances.

Feature 5: Predictive Maintenance

Implement predictive maintenance capabilities to proactively identify and address potential issues with the solar panel systems.

Feature 6: Enhanced Reporting and Analytics

Improve reporting and analytics capabilities to provide detailed insights into energy generation, savings, and system performance.

Feature 7: Mobile App Development

Develop a dedicated mobile application to offer a convenient and user-friendly experience on smartphones and tablets.

Feature 8: Integration with Third-Party Services

Integrate the dashboard with third-party services, such as weather APIs, financial systems, or energy market data, to enhance functionality and data accuracy.
